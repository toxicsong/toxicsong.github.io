Hip Hop Abs
Hip Hop Abs is a workout/dance routine that claims to significantly improve one’s abdominal area through lightweight hip-hop dance moves. Hip Hop Abs claims to be a relatively painless way to lose weight and sculpt abs; it advertises “you never do a single crunch.” In 2007 it was the highest-selling workout video in the United States. — from wikipedia
